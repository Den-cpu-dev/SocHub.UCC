# Department of Sociology & Anthropology - University of Cape Coast

Official website for the Department of Sociology & Anthropology at the University of Cape Coast, Ghana.

## About

This website provides comprehensive information about:

-   Undergraduate, Postgraduate, and Sandwich programs
-   Course descriptions and career pathways
-   Faculty profiles and research interests
-   SOASA (Sociology and Anthropology Students Association)
-   Academic resources and materials
-   Scholarship opportunities
-   Contact information
