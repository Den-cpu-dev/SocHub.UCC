About Our Department

The Department of Sociology and Anthropology is dedicated to understanding human behavior, social institutions, and cultural diversity. Our programs combine theoretical knowledge with practical research experience, preparing students for careers in education, government, non-profits, and private sectors.

We are committed to fostering critical thinking, cultural sensitivity, and research excellence among our students and faculty.
